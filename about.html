Alma Victor Events has built its reputation on delivering unique, unforgettable weddings and events to a discerning clientele. Whether you are looking for the intimacy and refinement of a first-class 40 guest wedding, or the sophistication and complexity of an elegant 400+ guest VIP event, we will work with you to create a custom experience unequalled in San Miguel de Allende, the destination wedding capital of Mexico.
At Alma Victor Events our clients always come first. We know that if you have chosen to host a destination wedding or event, you are looking for something special. San Miguel de Allende will certainly provide you with the magical setting you are looking for, and now you need the right planner to create the magical event you are looking for. That is where Alma Victor Events comes in, we make it our business to ensure that everything is just right and that the result is nothing short of spectacular.
Our goal is to deliver a unique, unforgettable experience for you and your guests. Your wedding or special event will be custom tailored to your specifications, reflecting your personality and tastes, while capturing the distinctive character and charm of San Miguel de Allende.
Alma Victor Events is a fully bilingual serving a domestic and international clientele. We are also an LGTB-friendly business.
Alma Caballero
Born in Mexico City but with family roots in San Miguel de Allende, Alma Caballero grew up traveling between the two cities. Today, she makes her home in San Miguel.
With 15+ years’ experience as an event professional, Alama is deeply conversant with all aspects of wedding and event planning: “No detail is too small. Our purpose is to create an extraordinary, memorable event for our clients”, says Alma. “We have everything covered, and so our clients can relax, enjoy and have the time of their lives”. 
Alma’s training as a successful wedding and event planner began at a very early age. As she likes to put it, “I grew up in the fiesta, event planning runs in my veins”.  Hosting VIP guests, artists, and world-class musicians was a regular feature of everyday life in the Caballero household, which gave Alma a head start when she decided to turn her passion for catering and event planning into a full-time business for high-end clients. 
Her natural talent and practical experience with top level events and clients are reinforced by thorough ABC endorsed training with some of the best wedding and event planners in the industry.
Throughout her career, Alma’s unique combination of creativity and capable professionalism has ensured a steady stream of domestic and international contracts and VIP events that have taken from San Miguel de Allende and Mexico City, to Europe and Africa.
Victor Hugo
Designer Victor Hugo is a master of creative direction. He draws on his rich cultural heritage, his natural talent and his extensive training to produce exquisite decorative creations and magical floral designs for weddings, events, shows, interior design projects and more.
Born in Nuevo Laredo, he grew up in Texas, a child of two cultures.  After studying Art History in both the United States and in Mexico he began his professional career at the prestigious World Floral Expo in New York and then spent the next 25+ years honing his craft around the country, from New York, to Miami, Houston, Dallas and San Antonio among others.  His creative talents are complemented by a natural flexibility, a positive disposition that speaks volumes to his success with clients:
“For me life is about beauty, I see beauty everywhere and I make it my mission to bring it into every aspect of my work”
Today Victor makes his home in the beautiful and historical city of San Miguel de Allende, Mexico.
Both as individuals and as Alma Victor Events, Alma and Victor are committed to giving back to the community and to supporting causes close to their hearts.
In addition to hosting cultural events such as free musical performances for youth and the elderly, Alma Victor Events has thrown its support behind Casa y Asequia A.C., a non-profit environmental organization headquartered in San Miguel de Allende. Most recently Casa y Asequia implemented a project based on its “Mexican Model for Sustainable Reforestation” with the help of volunteers from the Comunidad Otomí (the community of the indigenous Otomí people).
