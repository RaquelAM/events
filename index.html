The heart of Mexico
The heart is closely associated with San Miguel de Allende, the reason for this is not just religious, although the “Sacred Heart” is certainly key aspect of the heart symbol in San Miguel. In addition to its religious overtones the heart has also denotes San Miguel’s central geographical location at the heart of Mexico and its historical location at the heart of independence. Why choose San Miguel de Allende for your destination wedding
Long recognized world-wide, and in the United States in particular, as a culture and art center, San Miguel de Allende has been attracting art students and artists seeking inspiration and instruction since the late 1930’s. It all started in 1938, when Peruvian artist Felipe Cossio del Pomar opened San Miguel’s first art school, the Escuela Universitaria de Bellas Artes in the town center. It is still housed in the former convent where it was founded.
