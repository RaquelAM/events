My 6 Favorite Romantic Outtings in San Miguel de Allende by Victor Hugo 
            If you haven’t heard about why you should consider San Miguel de Allende for your destination wedding, I invite you to read this earlier post:  Why choose San Miguel de Allende for your destination wedding.
            
            Suffice to say, that San Miguel de Allende is the perfect city for couples who are looking for the ideal destination to celebrate their wedding, spend their honeymoon, renew their vows, or just plain surround themselves with romance. 

            As an incurable romantic myself, I am always looking for new and beautiful ways to experience the city I love. Today, I’ve decided to share 6 of my favorite romantic experiences in this enchanting, historical town. 

            1- A romantic Stroll through Parque Juarez.  Located within a 5 or 6 minute walk from the city center, this beautiful city park, with its luxuriant foliage, crisscrossing paths, bubbling fountains and birds singing in the trees above, has the ideal atmosphere for a romantic stroll.  It is an absolute must for couples in love.   Of course, first you may want to stop and pick up a couple of delicious ice cream cones from Plazuela de San Felipe, my favorite ice cream vendor in San Miguel de Allende. 

            2 – A romantic early-morning hot air balloon ride high above the historical city of San Miguel de Allende. Imagine getting up at the crack of dawn, climbing into a waiting car at the front of your hotel and driving off to a waiting hot air balloon only to be whisked away on a romantic aerial tour of the city and its outskirts.  You and the person you love will experience San Miguel de Allende from a completely new perspective as you observe the city and its surrounding countryside from high above.  Sipping champagne and looking down from the balloon’s basket, you will float together on a tour past historical colonial architecture, beautiful vineyards and farms, and the stunning high desert landscape.  Don’t miss this unique experience!

            3- A romantic meal at Moxi’s. Located within Hotel Matilda and considered one of Mexico’s top restaurants, Moxi boasts an amazing menu developed and executed by internationally renowned Chef Enrique Olvera and his team.  Like the food, the service is first-class and the atmosphere is chic and sophisticated.   An intimate dinner at Moxi  is a great  choice for a first date, an excellent start to a romantic weekend escape, and even the perfect setting for a marriage proposal! 
          
            4 – A romantic sunset at one of San Miguel’s excellent roof-top terraces.  What could be more romantic than watching a beautiful golden sunset with the one love? Sunsets in San Miguel de Allende are simply spectacular. There are a number of excellent terrace restaurants and bars where you can enjoy a cool margarita, tapas and even a delicious meal, all while watching an amazing kaleidoscope of changing colors and cloud patterns against infinite skies.  

            5 - A romantic danzón at the Jardín in the city’s main square.  San Miguel de Allende’s main square in front of the famous Parroquia church is where the action is.  Locals and visitors alike flock daily o the square’s central Jardín (Garden) to watch local street events, meet up with friends, or just to soak up the local atmosphere.  If you are lucky enough to be present for the monthly Sunday social you will witness and pay decide to participate in a danzón or cha-cha-cha.  It will be one of the most romantic experiences you can imagine. Watching the couples, both young and not so young, some skilled and some not so skilled, all enjoying each other and dancing together to the music has been described as one of the sweetest experiences in San Miguel de Allende and is certainly a favorite among visitors.

            6 - A romantic escape to La Santísima Trinidad.  This one is definitely my very favorite romantic spot in the area.  Just a 25 minute drive from the City of San Miguel de Allende, la Santísima Trinidad has all the elements for the PERFECT romantic day-trip or weekend escape.  A trip to la Santísima Trinidad engages ALL of the senses with its endless fields of lavender, olive groves, and vineyards; its excellent restaurant that serves local organic produce; its bicycle tours, walking tours and its trail rides on horseback. You can even float down the beautiful waterways in a rowboat if you like!  If you are concerned about getting stuck in an overcrowded tourist resort, or being disturbed by endless partying and noise, don’t. Your day trip will be stress-free and relaxing. And if you decide to stay over, the well-appointed and well-staffed boutique hotel has just a handful of rooms ensuring a unique experience and personalized attention. 
            I hope you enjoyed this personal perspective on experiencing romance in San Miguel de Allende. If you have anything to add or would like to share your own romantic San Miguel tale, we’d love to hear from you! Send us an email using our contact form (link) and we will be in touch!
